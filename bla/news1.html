Nancy Pelosi Becomes First Woman to Serve as U.S. House Speaker Retires from Congress

By BBCNN Staff | November 6, 2025



Nancy Pelosi, the first woman to hold the office of Speaker of the U.S. House of Representatives, has announced her retirement from Congress. Her tenure marked decades of service and historic achievements in U.S. politics.

"I will not be seeking re-election to Congress. With a grateful heart, I look forward to my final year of service," Pelosi said in a video posted on X.
